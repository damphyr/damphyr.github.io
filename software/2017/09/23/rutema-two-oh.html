Rutema 2.0.0!

23 Sep 2017

It has been over ten years since the initial version of rutema saw the light, nine since it hit 1.0, five since the last official update of the gem and a full two years since I rewrote the engine.

Fifteen release candidate versions and - at long last - bugfixes and contributions by other people!

There is only one thing to say about this code: It is stable.

It has been filed down over the years, it has been in constant use for the entirety of its existence and it has been a tremendously useful tool in realizing highly complex test automation scenarios.

Also: oh my god, it has been ten years!!!
